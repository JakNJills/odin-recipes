Pad Gaprao (Pad Kra Pao)

Spicy Thai Basil Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Pad Gaprao (Pad Kra Pao) is a famous and popular Thai street food dish. A chicken stir-fry dish usually served over rice and topped with a fried egg. Pad Gaprao is believed to have originated from the Northern Region of Thailand. This dish rose in popularity around the world for it's simplicity to make and wonderful taste. Pad Krapow takes 10 to 15 minutes of prep time and roughly 10 to 15 minutes to make. A total of 20 to 30 minutes is all it takes to cook this amazing traditional Thai Street Food Dish.

Ingredients

Enough for 2 Servings

  • 5-10 Thai Chilies(to taste)
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • Vegetable Oil (as needed)
  • 1 spur chilies(Prik Chi Fah) or another mild red pepper
    • Chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tsp Fish Sauce
  • 1/2 Small Onion
    • Diced
  • 1/3 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 1 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Dark Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tsp White Sugar
  • 300g Chicken
    • Coarsely Ground
  • 1 Tsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 Cup of Long Bean
    • Cut into short pieces
  • 1 1/2 Cup Fresh Holy Basil Leaves
  • 2 Tbsp of Water
  • Jasmine Rice enough per serving
  • 2-3 Eggs

Steps

  1. Making the Sauce
    1. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, fish sauce, chicken broth, oyster sauce, white sugar, and brown sugar.
    2. Stir until the sugars are dissolved. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the Stir Fry
    1. :Mince the garlic and Thai bird's eye chilies. Set aside.
    2. Cook the Chicken: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and chilies, stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the ground chicken to the skillet, breaking it apart with a spatula. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
    3. Add the Sauce: Pour the prepared sauce over the cooked chicken in the skillet. Stir well to combine and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
    4. Add the Holy Basil: Turn off the heat and stir in the fresh holy basil leaves. The residual heat will wilt the basil. Mix well to combine.
    5. Prepare the Rice: While cooking the chicken, prepare your jasmine rice according to package instructions if you haven't done so already.
    6. Fry the Egg: In a separate small pan, heat a little oil over medium-high heat. Crack an egg into the pan and fry until the edges are crispy but the yolk is still runny (or cook to your preference).
    7. Assemble the Dish: Serve the chicken stir-fry over a bed of cooked jasmine rice. Top with the fried egg.
    8. Enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade Pad Gaprao!
